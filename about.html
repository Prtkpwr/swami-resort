Swami Resort is situated in Tarkarli village of Malvan Taluka in Sindhudurg District of Konkan. The resort is located close to the beach. Swami Resort is one of it's kind resort in Tarkarli. The resort was established by Mr. Hirlekar four years ago. The guests leave this place with happy hearted because of scenic nature, beauty of coconut trees and beaches. The people impressed specially by our service.

It's a resort in which you feel like you are at your home. The service provided is very special. We have Deluxe and AC-Deluxe rooms to best suit your need. The rooms are located just aside the beach. We have AC rooms as well as non-AC rooms. The rooms are coupled with Sofas, Chairs and TV. You can avail 24-hours hot and cold water there. We have 2 kitchens. There is shank in the middle.
